Sync card text with the latest deck source, preserving all review progress.

Scheduling Options

  • New cards/day — Maximum new cards introduced per day.
  • Learning steps — Intervals (in minutes) for cards still in the learning phase. Comma-separated.
  • Graduating interval — First review interval once a card leaves learning (Good vs Easy).
  • Starting ease — Initial ease factor for new cards (2.5 = default Anki).
  • Max reviews/day — Cap on review cards shown per day.
  • Easy/Hard multiplier — Scales the next interval when you press Easy or Hard.
  • Max review interval — Longest interval a card can reach (days).
  • Interval fuzz — Adds small random variation to intervals to prevent card clustering.
  • Relearn steps — Re-learning intervals (minutes) after a lapse.
  • New interval after lapse — Multiplier applied to the old interval on a lapse (0 = reset to 1 day).
  • Leech threshold — Lapses before a card is flagged as a leech.
  • Leech action — Tag the card or suspend it when the threshold is reached.
